Meet the Founders

Michael Levey

Michael runs the day-to-day operations of Speaking Office, including overseeing the management, development and strategy of the Speaking Office roster. Prior to co-founding the company, Michael was a commercial litigation lawyer at a leading London law firm. His experience within this field has played an integral role in shaping his approach to client and business management.

Outside of Speaking Office, Michael has a son , is an avid supporter of Newcastle United and volunteers weekly at a charity supporting disabled swimmers.

Nick Gold

Nick has been working in the speaking marketplace for over 10 years. He is the MD of Speakers Corner – one of Europe’s leading speaker bureaus - and President of the International Association of Speakers Bureaus.

Consequently, he spends much of his time meeting some of the most thought-provoking speakers in the world, and is passionate about the effect they can have on an audience. For Nick, co-founding Speaking Office meant creating a platform where brilliant thoughts and ideas could be shared, benefitting anyone who wanted to learn something extraordinary.

Outside Speaking Office, Nick is married, has two daughters, is on the board of a restaurant group, and serves as a trustee for a charity in Camden.

Tim Gold

Tim is a member of the Speaking Office board, lending his expertise in an advisory capacity to the team.

Tim is also the co-owner and operations’ director at Speakers Corner, overseeing the 1,000 plus speaking engagements they work on every year. He still manages the majority of the company’s key accounts and is driven to maintain the 20% year-on-year growth achieved over the last five years.

In Tim’s spare time, he is an avid follower of Arsenal football club, student of the Catalan language, a keen fan of food (mostly pizza) and enjoys the occasional stint on a cross trainer.
